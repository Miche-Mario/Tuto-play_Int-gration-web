WHAT ARE TUTOS ?

Shorthand for tutorials, they are learning guides available in paper format or on the internet and consisting of visual instructions (photos, videos) showing the user how to perform a specific task in a wide array of fields such as creative hobbies, DIY, beauty, cooking, photography. Tutorials have grown as the go-to training and self-education tool nowadays.
Internet and the presence of social media in our daily lives has made it possible for tutorials to be the gateway to free education for everyone.

CULTURE - COMMUNITY - MEDIAS - FOOD - FASHION - BEAUTY - FINANCE - SPORT - TOURISME…

Whatever the field chosen, tutorial creators (aka Tuto Makers) will compete over a “tutorials battle” resulting in finding out the one among them who has the best tutorial making strategy.
Criteria:

  • You are a creator of tutorials relevant to the needs an African audience, no matter the field;
  • You own a Youtube channel;
  • You record your videos in HD/li>
Digital communities will be involved by voting for the best based on criteria such as: :
  • How relevant the content is to their needs;
  • How clear the speaker sounds;
  • How originally the tutorial is presented.

The video which gathers the most views will win the “BEST TUTO of the continent” title and be awarded with :
prizes including coaching to become a certified influencer and twelve (12) professional videos produced by UbizOM5 channel.
WE HAVE GOT INFLUENCERS AS COACHES …
An influencer is a person renowned on the internet and or for their expertise in some given field. Their passion for everything social media is also noticeable.

That person is perceived as a thought leader, a role-model and is trusted by his or her subscribers.
The influencer shares useful product recommendations with his or her community which he or she looks after to keep.
